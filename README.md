# TimeFormatter
Tired of having to use colons, new lines, and "AM/PM" when entering times on multiple lines on sheets/excel? With this program you can now save a few seconds.

Sample input/output:
1040p 11 1130 12 1230 1 200 3 +1
10:41 PM
11:01 PM
11:31 PM
12:01 AM
12:31 AM
1:01 AM
2:01 AM
3:01 AM
7 8 9 10 11 12a 1 2
7:00 AM
8:00 AM
9:00 AM
10:00 AM
11:00 AM
12:00 AM
1:00 AM
2:00 AM
